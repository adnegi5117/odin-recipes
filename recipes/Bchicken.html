Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken, traditionally known as murgh makhani, is an Indian dish originating in New Delhi.


It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture.
It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste.

Ingredient

  • Butter (100g)
  • Chicken (1kg)
  • Magic Powers
  • Some Indian Spices
  • Love

Steps:

  1. Marinade the chicken with Indian Spices.
  2. Then cook the chicken.
  3. Use Magic Powers to form the gravy and put the chicken in it.
  4. Add 100g of Butter and cook it to perfection.
  5. Add some Love.
